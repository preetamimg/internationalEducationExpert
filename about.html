STUDY MBBS IN ABROAD
Why Study MBBS Abroad?
IEE is an Overseas Education Consultancy that provides expert advice and guidance to students who want to pursue MBBS abroad. With our experienced international education specialists, you can easily unlock your dream of studying MBBS abroad.
We provide our students with intellectual opinion on anything related to studying MBBS Abroad with utmost generosity. Our experienced counselors are well-versed with the entire procedure and have provided services to more than 500+ students so far.
IEE provides one-stop solutions for all your queries related to overseas education. From providing information on the top medical universities around the world to helping you with the admission process and visa formalities, our team will provide comprehensive support throughout your journey.
At IEE, we understand that every student has different needs and requirements for their overseas education goals. That’s why we offer personalized service which is tailored specifically for you based on your academic goals and budget.
Our Vision
At IEE our vision is to turn your dreams and aspirations into reality. We strive to reach out to aspiring students and create awareness about the education opportunities available in different parts of the world.

Services At IEE
Ensure Education is an initiative to assist at each and every level possible during and after admission into any college for MBBS aspirants who aspire to study medicine from abroad . Our endeavour is to be a small part in the students journey to fulfill their dream of becoming a doctor.
Customized Counseling
Ensure Education not only focus on the admission process instead help each individual to let understand the concept and complete process of admission.Regardless of MBBS course we offer free counselling session for students to choose what best suits for their career
Shortlisting University
Ensure Education provide all students with large platform where student have great number of options when it comes to university selection in multiple countries and guide each candidate to select best university available to meet their expectation.
Admission Assistance
Ensure Education assist students during the complete admission process .Our team of experts are always available to guide candidate regarding each and every single document required for and during the procedure. In case students are not able to provide few required documents at the given time , we are there to help them out for every solution possible in India as well as outside wherever the student will get enrolled.
Visa Guidance
Ensure Education guide students thoroughly in which they will be providing guidance regarding admission as well as visa process. Documents required for visa , biometrics assistance or the time taken or delayed during and after will be managed by our team at the same time candidate will be acknowledge with each and every information asked at the time.
On-Arrival Assistance
Ensure Education consultancy provides a complete package for students , we are providing best for the students and give them complete assistance on-arrival. We help students till they reach hostel from airport as well as provide all the basic amenities required.
FMGE/NExT Coaching
Ensure Education endeavour to give best for the students during and after the completion of their MBBS course . Most of the universities partner with IEE provides FMGE/NExT Coaching during the final year of their course which make it reliable for the students willing to work in INDIA after completing course from any abroad country.
