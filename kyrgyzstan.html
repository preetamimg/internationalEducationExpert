Kyrgyzstan
Study MBBS in Kyrgyzstan for Indian Students, Fees & Admission
MBBS from Kyrgyzstan
provides a large platform for medical aspirants who were having dream of becoming future doctor. It is an landlocked country situated in Asian continent which is only 2324 km away from India and the less distance from India makes the country one of the priority option for Indian students to choose it and the time contrast between two countries is just 30 minutes.
Most of the MBBS colleges are situated in and near to Bishkek, which is the capital city of country. Students pursuing Medical from Kyrgyzstan never came across any language barrier as the classes of MBBS courses are conducted in the English language which is suitable for Medical students from any corner of the world with the duration of 5 to 6 years depending upon the universities. The candidates have to undergo theoretical medical studies for five years and one year for practical experience. Medical colleges of Kyrgyzstan are globally acknowledged due to a high-standard of education structure, college infrastructure, low-tuition fess along with the favourable climatic conditions which make it more suitable for students for long run.
For Admission in any university of the country, the students should qualify NEET examination in their required category and must have passed higher education i.e 10+2 with a minimum of 55% percentage marks. WHY MBBS in Kyrgyzstan?
Choosing Kyrgyzstan is never restricted as an option as it always remain the first choice among the Indian student when it comes to pursue MBBS Form Abroad There are several reasons why a candidate should select MBBS in Kyrgyzstan Some of them are as follows:
  1. MBBS in Kyrgyzstan is open for national as well as International medical aspirants.
  2. Universities of Kyrgyzstan offer clinical and PG programs to the students while studying medicine in Kyrgyzstan.
  3. There is no complicated procedure adopted by medical universities of Kyrgyzstan for MBBS admission in Kyrgyzstan .
  4. No entrance test is required for seeking MBBS admission in Kyrgyzstan.
  5. Degree from any universities of Kyrgyzstan offer great international exposure for medical students around the world.
  6. The cost of living is economical for medical students pursuing MBBS from Kyrgyzstan.
  7. Favourable climatic conditions make it more suitable for students to study with ease.
Eligibility Criteria for MBBS in Kyrgyzstan
Some of the Qualifications for pursuing an MBBS in Kyrgyzstan are as follows:
  1. Students’ age eligibility of 17-25 years.
  2. Meet the criteria of admission by 50% in 12th (General Category) and 40% in 12th (SC/ST).
  3. Necessary subjects for entry are Physics, Chemistry, and Biology in 12th grade.
  4. Financial background of guardians must be solid enough to cover the entire course fees of MBBS in Kyrgyzstan.
Therefore, if you want to get admission to top universities in Kyrgyzstan for MBBS, make sure you qualify for the criteria eligibility.

Required Documents for MBBS in Kyrgyzstan:
Following are the documents which are mandatory during admission process in any university of Kyrgyzstan.
  1. Scorecard of NEET ( Candidate must have qualified NEET exam ).
  2. Marksheet of secondary (10th) and higher secondary education (12th).
  3. Transfer Certificate ( TC ) from the higher school.
  4. Character certificate from last school ( If required ).
  5. The candidate must have a school-leaving certificate.
  6. Photocopy of passport and passport size photos.
  7. Bank copy of the guardians or candidate indicating they have sufficient funds to pay university fees.

Admission Process for MBBS in Kyrgyzstan
Kyrgyzstan’s admissions process is straightforward and significantly less time-consuming. Kyrgyzstan medical universities follow the standard admissions procedure. Here are some important points about the Kyrgyzstan admission process.
  1. Documents and the online application form must be filled out by students.
  2. After verifying the scanned documents, the universities send the admission letter to shortlisted students.
  3. Your admission letter must be approved by the NMC.
  4. In order to qualify, students must take an online English proficiency test through Skype or Zoom.
  5. Universities accept tuition fees and admission fees by bank transfer.
  6. IEE will submit the student’s VISA application after admission is confirmed.
  7. The approval process for a VISA takes between 30 and 45 days.
